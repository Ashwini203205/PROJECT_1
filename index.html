Our Story

About Artisan Gallery

Where passion for art meets exceptional curation

A Decade of Artistic Excellence

Founded in 2014, Artisan Gallery began as a small studio in Manhattan's Chelsea art district. Today, we've grown into one of the world's leading online art platforms, connecting discerning collectors with extraordinary artists.

Our team of expert curators travels the globe to discover emerging talent and established masters alike. Every artwork in our collection is hand-selected, authenticated, and comes with a certificate of provenance.

We believe art should be accessible, yet exceptional. That's why we offer flexible payment plans, complimentary art advisory services, and a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on every purchase.
